EDUBOTS

Chatbots in Higher Education

In the EDUBOTS project we aim to increase student retention by automating pedagogical feedback for educators and giving personalised follow ups to students.

We believe scalable, publicly available findings can improve the quality of higher education in Europe

Our main goals

We have big dreams

Increased retention

We expect to be able to document lower dropout rates through increased student engagement
Improved feedback

We focus on increasing the frequency and accuracy of feedback between educator and student
Increased student engagement

We want our work to have proven increase in student engagement. We focus on engagement between peers/educators and chatbots
Knowledge sharing

We make all findings publicly available. All documents are open source to create a sharing culture in academics

Target audience

We ❤️ students and teachers

We help students

The core of the project is to reduce dropout rates by increasing student engagement using pedagogical chatbots for higher education.

This means improved learning outcomes and more students that succeed in their studies - a huge socio-economic benefit for Europe.

We help teachers

The Edubots project will contribute to breaking down borders and building up new forms of collaboration.

We go beyond the MOOCs, to stimulate collaboration among academics in open learning communities and making best practices publicly available
